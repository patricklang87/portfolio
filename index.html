Patrick Lang's Digital Portfolio


I began teaching myself how to code in June 2020.

This is my digital portfolio, designed to keep track of projects I develop as I practice programming.

I began by practicing HTML, CSS and JavaScript and have gradually spent more time practicing Python as well, primarily motivated through my interest in its potential for Natural Language Processing.

None of the projects are polished, final versions. They are sketches, generally begun and interrupted as I discovered new tools available to me.

I work as a college-level instructor of German and Russian. Inspired by our online course management tool, Sakai, and its auto-graded testing functions, I am gradually working toward the ability to create auto-graded testing functions customized more specifically to my goals.
