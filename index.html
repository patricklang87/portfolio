Patrick Lang's Digital Portfolio


I am an instructor of German and Russian at the college level and began teaching myself how to code in June 2020.

This is my digital portfolio, designed to keep track of projects I develop as I practice programming.

I began by practicing HTML, CSS and JavaScript and have gradually spent more time practicing Python as well, primarily motivated through my interest in its potential for Natural Language Processing.

To provide this page with a visual framework, I have integrated my own paintings and artwork into its design.

None of the projects are polished, final versions. They are sketches, generally begun and interrupted as I discovered new opportunities available to me.
